Dungeons

This season, Brewmaster is looking really strong in all of the dungeons. There aren't really any scary tank busters, making this season much more relaxed. In this section of the guide, we will go over how strong we are in each dungeon, the routes for each dungeon, aswell as tricks for difficult bosses.

Bosses

This section will go over the difficulty of each boss as a Brewmaster. It will discuss tips and tricks if there are any, as well as specific defensive rotations for survivng difficult tank busters. Because we are so early into the new expansion, there is not much to be said here.

General Umbriss

Medium

There aren't really any tricks for this boss. Just make sure that you have a defensive for Skullsplitter, as it can hurt quite a bit if you don't.

Chopper Redhook

Easy

This boss is very easy. You want to try and bait the frontals from the adds away from the boss, that way they don't hit your party when he pulls them in. You can also use a defensive to soak a bomb if your party will not be able to kite him into them soon enough.

Ingra Maloch

Easy

This boss is a complete joke for tanks. You want to try and cycle your interrupt with the rest of your party for Spirit Bolt, and that's it.

Blightbone

Medium

Blightbone is a weird boss to decide the difficulty of. If you pull a bunch of trash with him, then he is on the tougher side to live. If you don't, however, he is pretty easy to live. He has a pretty hard hitting tank buster, called Crunch, which needs a defensive, especially if there are other mobs hitting you at the same time. Other than that, there isn't really anything special for this boss.

Avanoxx

Medium

This boss's tank beater can do a lot damage, especially if you don't have a defensive for it. You want to make sure that no Starved Crawlers can touch the boss, as this will enrage her, increasing her damage done by 50%. Luckily, Ring of Peace is a great tool for this.

Orator Krix'vizk

Medium

Orator Krix'vizk isn't too hard, so long as you are careful. You want to make sure you have a defensive for each tank buster, Subjugate, and that you are not moving the boss around too much. If you pull the boss too for away from a party member, they will take a lot of damage.

Speaker Shadowcrown

Medium

This boss requires a lot of movement, mainly through the use of skyriding. The only somewhat difficult part for Brewmaster, however, is the tank buster Obsidian Beam. It hits for a good amount of magic damage, requiring a defensive for each one.

E.D.N.A.

Medium

This boss's mechanics are very simple. You want to make sure that you have a defensive for each tank buster, and that you are cycling defensives where needed for the constant ticking damage.
