Dungeons

This season, Brewmaster is looking really strong in all of the dungeons. There aren't really any scary tank busters, making this season much more relaxed. In this section of the guide, we will go over how strong we are in each dungeon, the routes for each dungeon, aswell as tricks for difficult bosses.

Dungeon Difficulties

None of the dungeons are really hard for Brewmaster, and with them being so new, there is not much to say regarding them.

Operation Mechagon: Workshop

Easy
